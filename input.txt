In recent years, the phrase "Black Lives Matter" (BLM) has resonated across the globe, sparking a renewed interest in racial justice and equality. Originating in the United States, the Black Lives Matter movement emerged in response to a series of unjust killings of Black individuals, particularly at the hands of law enforcement. The movement, however, has since evolved into a powerful call for dismantling systemic racism and challenging centuries of racial oppression.
